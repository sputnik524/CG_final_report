1. Theme and Motivation


Architecture and Morality, 2004



Surreale Mann Kopf in der cloud - Stock-Fotografie



Topic for this year's rendering competition is "out of place", which describes a scenario that something in a inappropriate position or something messy and disarranged.

My idea is inspried by these two surreal-style images above, where some natural things (a bouquet of flowers, fog) come out of a human's head. First one is an oil paint called "Architecture and Morality" from artist Gleen Brown. My ideal outcome would be a combination of these two images in a proper and aesthetic way. In particular, I want to construct an indoor scene includes a garmented person's boby with his head replaced by some fancy flowers, surrounded by some faint mist.

This ideal result will be consistent with the topic "out of place" in some respects. Visually, the natural objects come out of nowhere and somehow replace the human's head. Conceptually, this picture indicates a human's disarranged and messy mind.



2. Results

Inspired by the idea above, I want to construct an open scene that uses a dimmed, obsolete city as the background. The main component of the scene would be a big sci-fi tree with mysterious glow that suddenly comes out of nowhere and interrupts the city. The tree is surrounded by abstract and giant elements that is agnostic to the people in the city. Subsequently, people nearby are influenced by the tree and start transforming their figure. Bouquet of flowers come out of their head and take their body over....

scene construction

Contructing from scratch, a cluster of city buildings meshes are placed at the back of the init plane as the overall background of the whole scene.


Then the tree is placed with certian landscape attached and some giant dielectric plane as a division. Besides, to create the dimmed atomsphere, a homogenerous medium is added.


Then, some abstract mesh elements and area lights are created surrounding the main tree, which makes the tree looks more mysterious


Too create more visual impact, some fancy staffs are added. Particularly, a floating particle system that mimics a comtinuous flow from the root of the tree, glowing in light purple. Seconly, a wireframe icoshpere is created with geometry frame, which also make the scene looks more complex.



Then, main characters are introduced with their meshes pre-edited with their heads replaced by flowers. Both meshes are downloaded and I did the editting part.
